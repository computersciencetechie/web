Introduction

Vegetable Masala Maggi is a quick & easy Indian snack made by tossing maggi noodles along with mixed vegetables and spices. This variation of maggi noodles is healthy and a tasty twist to the simple & plain version due to nutritive value of fresh vegetables added to this dish. The veggies are saut√©ed in a pan along with the maggi noodles which imparts a unique taste to the noodles as well. Vegetable Masala Maggi is not only a easy to make food but also one of the most yummy and tangy dish loved by people of all ages and especially by the children.
